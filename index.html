What is it?

Edison Garage is ICO-ISED's new internship program created to enable students reach their full potential. We aim to help students grow as professionals by providing them with the necessary tools and resources. Because students will work independently, the department can avoid long on-boarding processes and significant resource allocation. It will also allow students to spend more productive time on their project and less time integrating.

ISED

what's in it for ISED?

Choosing From The Best

Work terms are a great opportunity for student to learn and show their talents to potential employers. ISED can use this as an in-depth interview opportunity to see the potential of students and make a more informed decision when hiring.

Having proven new hires with a wider array of skills will also reduce the department's dependence on consultants to try new technologies.

Let The People Know

ISED is an exciting place to work! The Edison Garage gives ISED a platform to provide students with an insight into the industry grade work being done here. Let's break the stigma and show the next generation of professionals the modern and versatile DSD

Experiment

The world of technology is always changing with new technologies are emerging every day. The Edison Garage will be a sandbox for students to try new technologies and an opportunity for the department to gain insight into emerging technologies.

With this program we hope to bring more excitement, ideas, and positive change to the department.

Lower Average Age

To remain competitive, most workplaces maintain an age-diverse workforce that is able to rapidly adapt with market changes. Currently, the average age of the CS group remains at 47.6. In comparison, the average age of employees at Google and Facebook does not exceed 29.

The Edison Garage program hopes to introduce new young talent to the department while providing a opportunity for students to learn and grow in a professional environment

Our Vision

V1.0

As an initial trial, Edison Garage will launch with only 3 students. The students will be provided with modern workstations, smart boards, and collaboration areas to encourage team work.

The proposed time-line of the project will be as follows:

  • Pick a Project

    choose their project based on a given theme

  • Project Proposal

    prepare a proposal to present their plan

    • describe the idea
    • divide out tasks and responsibilities
    • create a time-line
    • request resources

  • Code!

    students must document work, communicate within their team, seek help, and find resources

  • Present the Project

    by the end of the 4 month term, students must present their project to the department.

    • answer questions
    • defend choices
    • identify lessons & mistakes

  • More in V2.0

V2.0

Based on the success of V1.0, we hope to expand the Garage into multiple teams and across other departments. We would also like to make the projects interdisciplinary with developers, business students, and UX designers working on the same team.

The final showcase will also expand to have judges that will rank the projects and one project will be selected as the winner.

Edison Garage aims to provide a priceless opportunity to participants and the department.
