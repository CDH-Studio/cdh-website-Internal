Welcome To Edison Garage!
ISED Intern Program
Tell Me More

4 Months



The interns must design and present their completed projects within 4 months.

3 Students



3 of the top applicants are chosen to build the strong team to complete the project together.

1 Project



A a general topic will be provided but students will have complete freedom.

Whats in it for the Students?

This program provides a unique opportunity for students to grow their projects from an idea to reality. They will have the freedom to select their project and the technologies that best meet their needs. Once completed, students will prepare a presentation and will showcase their work to the department.

As the students develop their project, they will learn the professional requirements of creating project proposals, completing requirement solicitation, writing documentation, and presenting your completed work.

If you are a well-rounded, self-driven, and love to code then the Edison Garage is for you!

Whats in it for ISED?

Lower Average Age

To remain competitive all workplaces need to have an age-diverse workforce that is able to rapidly adapt with market changes. Currently, the average age of the CS group remains at 47.6. In comparison, the average age of employees at Google and facebook does not exceed 29.

The Edison Garage program hopes to introduce new young talent to the department while providing a opportunity for students to learn and grow in a professional environment

Choosing From The Best

Work terms are a great opportunity for student to learn and show their talents to potential employers. ISED can use this as an in-depth interview opportunity to see the potential of students and make a more informed decision when hiring.

Having proven new hires with a wider array of skill-sets will also reduce the department's dependence on consultants to use new technologies.

Let The People Know

ISED is an exciting place to work! The Edison Garage give ISED a great platform to give students an insight about the industry grade work being done here. Lets break the stigma and show the next generation of professionals the modern and versatile DSD

New Insight

The world of technology is always changing with new technologies emerging everyday. The Edison Garage will be a sandbox for students to try new techlogoes and an opportunity for the department to gain insight into emerging technologies.

With this program we hope to bring more excitement, ideas, and positive change to the department.

The Vision

Today (V1.0)

In the trial stage, the Edison Garage will launch with only 3 students. The students will be provided with modern workstations, smart boards, and collaboration areas to encourage team work.

The proposed time-line of the project will be as follows:

  • Pick a Project

    choose their project based on a given theme

  • Project Proposal

    prepare a proposal to present their plan

    • describe the idea
    • divide out tasks and responsibilities
    • create a time-line
    • request resources

  • Code!

    Throughout the project students must document work, communicate within their team, seek help, and find resources

  • Present the Project

    By the end of the 4 month term, students must present their project to the department.

    • answer questions
    • defend choices
    • identify lessons & mistakes

  • More to come in V2.0
