What is CDH Studio?

CDH Studio is a 16-week internship program that acts as a sandbox for talented students to reach their full potential. In small teams with mentors, students will work independently with leading technology on business innovation. The team’s challenge is to develop a product from ideation to release at the end of their work term. The teams will also make a formal presentation to a group of Senior Executives.

Spread The Word

ISED is an exciting place to work! The CDH Studio offers students an insight into the interesting work being done in ISED to support the Department’s mandate. Let's break the stigma and show the next generation of IT professionals the modern and versatile ISED.

Experiment

We are living in a constant state of change with new technology emerging every day. The CDH Studio will be a sandbox for students to play with new technologies and an opportunity for students to gain insight into emerging technologies.

Mentorship

Teams will be paired with volunteer mentors allowing students and mentors to learn and grow together. This will enable and encourage bidirectional knowledge transfer between the students and mentors.

The Project

Theme

The theme for winter 2019 is data analytics, machine learning and chatbots. Selected students will work in a team to propose and implement an idea within the time and theme provided.

Timeline

The proposed timeline of the project will be as follows:

  • Pick a Project

    Students select their project on a pre-selected topic related to IT renewal

  • Project Proposal

    Students prepare a project proposal

    • describe the idea
    • divide out tasks and responsibilities
    • create project timeline
    • plan 2-week sprints

  • Code!

    Students must document work, communicate within their team, seek help, and find resources

  • Present the Project

    Students prepare a formal presentation and present their project to DSD Management.

    • answer questions
    • defend choices
    • identify lessons learned

  • All Done!
